About elementary.

elementary OS is a Linux distribution, based on Ubuntu, famous with its own unique style and design.

It includes the beautiful Pantheon desktop environment, and a number of in-house apps, that aims to set the standard for good design & user experience in an operating system.

Whether you're a Linux newbie or a seasoned Linux veteran, elementary OS is the perfect choice for you.

Get elementary.

elementary OS is completely free. You can install it on as many computers as you'd like or even copy it and give it away to your friends. Feel free to donate some cash to the developers, but that's not required.
